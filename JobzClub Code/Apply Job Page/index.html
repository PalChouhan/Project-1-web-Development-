JobzClub

Our business is a Jobs Management System used to manage the jobs posted by different companies for different vacancies. It also allows job seekers to view different jobs posted by companies and can apply to those jobs with their resumes. It is a good platform for companies and job seekers to post and apply for jobs at one place. Companies can view the applications of different applicants and shortlist them on basis of criteria. Also, company can arrange interview appointment with shortlisted applicant.
